1. Navigation:

•   Navigation Bar:   Directly below the header, you have a navigation bar with options.

•   Drive:   If you want to become a driver.

•   Business:   For business-related services.

•   About:   To learn more about the company.

•   Language Options:   You can switch the language of the website by clicking on the language code ("EN" for English in this case). The Google Translate widget in the footer provides additional language options.






2. Book Your Ride:

•   Form:   The main section of the webpage contains a form titled "Book Your Ride." Here, you can input details such as the pickup location, destination, date, and time for your ride.

•   Submit:   After entering the necessary details, click the "Book Now" button to submit your ride request.






3. Footer:

•   PD server:   Go to first page.

•   Visit Help Center:   Link to the help center for assistance.

•   Company:   Information about the company, including sections like About Us, Our Offerings, Newsroom, Investors, Blog, and Careers.

•   Ride:   Links related to ride services.

•   Travel:   Links related to travel services.






4. Language Translation:

•   The Google Translate widget in the footer allows you to translate the content into different languages. Click on the language code to access language options.






5. Explore and Experiment:

•   Click on different links, try booking a ride, and explore the different sections of the website.
